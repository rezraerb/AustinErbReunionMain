Descendants of Austin Erb

This site lists information about the descendants of Austin B. Erb, 1872-1953. Descendants of his children Leo, Ira, Iva, Roy, and Jay, attend an annual Erb family reunion near Clarksville, Michigan.

This site also lists partial information for the ancestors of Austin, back to the late 15th century in the Emmental region of the Canton of Bern, in Switzerland. The earliest known named ancestor is Ulrich Erb, known from his childrens' christening records from the chruch of Wuerzbrunnen. Unfortunately, the prior church was burned in a fire in 1494, along with its records, and it is unlikely that earlier records for the Erb family will be found.

I hope this site is helpful for the Erbs who descended from Austin, and ask that any with additional information or corrections please contact Ezra at ezra.erb@gmail.com

Thank you!
